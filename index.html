Work Experience

Pishtazan Andisheh Pouya, Iran (January 2015 - June 2015)

Pishtazan Andisheh Pouya is located in Iran and provides IT services to other companies. My responsibilities as a network specialist were to setup a reliable LAN network and making sure that it was working properly. We had two Proliant HP servers and we were experiencing delays in our inbound and outbound traffic. I investigated the cause and found that the resources available to the servers were not being utilised efficiently due to the lack of a proper load balancing policy on the singular port in use (out of several available). Having used virtualisation previously, I conducted some experiments on my laptop and based on the results suggested we could virtualise our servers to have a more efficient network. After this, I installed ESX5.5 and created the VMs. I installed Microsoft server 2012 on them an added various services and roles like Active Directory, DNS, DHCP. Some branches were located out of town (e.g a warehouse), hence I added the RRAS role and set up a VPN server so that users from these remote offices could connect securely to our network. Kaspersky security center and Kerio control were installed to control and monitor the network and users' security and internet usage management respectively (importing groups and users from Active Directory).
